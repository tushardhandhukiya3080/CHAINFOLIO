Learn Web3 by building

Understand Web3 from the ground up.

Web3 is the next phase of the internet — one where users own their data, assets, and identity instead of renting them from big platforms. This site walks you through the core ideas, one concept at a time, with live interactive demos so theory turns into intuition.

Web1 → Web2 → Web3

The clearest way to "get" Web3 is to see what came before it.

📖

Web1 · Read

The early internet (1990s). Static pages you could only read. Few creators, many readers.

✍️

Web2 · Read + Write

Today's internet. You create content on platforms (YouTube, Instagram) — but the platform owns the data and the rules.

🔑

Web3 · Read + Write + Own

You truly own your assets and identity via blockchain. No single company controls the network.

Why learn this now?

You don't need to be a trader or a crypto enthusiast to benefit. The same primitives that power Web3 — verifiable ownership, programmable money, and trustless coordination — are showing up in finance, gaming, identity, and supply chains. Understanding the foundations now means you can evaluate the hype, spot real use cases, and build on top of it.
